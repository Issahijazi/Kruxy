About Kruxy

Kruxy is a Lebanese industrial company, a pioneer in the manufacture and packaging, and marketing of all types of nuts and its derivatives, packaging of all types of dried fruit, and coffee trade in all its forms, in addition to manufacturing, packaging all kinds and types of chocolate, and providing the best luxury chocolate for all occasions.
